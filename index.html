Welcome to Michael's Pages.

This tool convert serial communication traffic to pcapng that could be read and analyzed by Wireshark.

Wirtten by AutoHotKey scripts.

Currently supported protocol:

1) DNP3.0

2) IEC 60870-5-101

3) IEC 60870-5-103

4) IEC 60870-5-104

5) Modbus TCP
